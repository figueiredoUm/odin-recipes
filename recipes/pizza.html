Pepperoni Pizza

Most of us know how to make a basic pizza (if you don't, you should), but this recipe will surprise you on how special and refined it is.

Ingredients:

  • 1kg of flour
  • 10g of dry yeast
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 cup of soy oil
  • 250g of mozzarela cheese
  • 1/2 cup of tomato sauce
  • 30g of pepperoni
  • 10g of basil leaves
  • 10g of oregano
  1. To prepare the dough, first start by mixing the flour and dry yeast in a bowl;
  2. Then add water and oil;
  3. After the dough is homogeneous, leave it to rest for about 1 hour;
  4. After 1 hour has passed, roll the dough into a medium-sized circle;
  5. Spread the tomato sauce onto the dough and add the cheese on top
  6. Now add the toppings;
  7. Lastly put the pizza in the oven and bake it for about 35-45 minutes;
