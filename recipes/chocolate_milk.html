Chocolate Milk

Recipe

Most of us know how to make a basic chocolate milk (if you don't, you should), but this recipe will surprise you on how special and refined it is.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of the chocolate powder of choice

Preparation:

  1. To prepare milk, pour it in a cup;
  2. Then the chocolate;
  3. Mix them thoroughly;
  4. Let it rest for about 15 seconds;
  5. Serve.
